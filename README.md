# yield-optimizer-protocol
A non-custodial yield optimization protocol designed to maximize capital efficiency across ecosystem networks.
## What we are building:
A non-custodial yield optimization protocol that helps retail and institutional holders maximize capital efficiency across ecosystem networks.

## Why issue a Stack Token on HACD:
The HACD incubator offers the exact network effects and builder support we need to scale safely. Issuing a Stack Token on HACD gives us a compliant, native foundation to coordinate protocol governance, distribute platform fees to our community, and secure early liquidity.
